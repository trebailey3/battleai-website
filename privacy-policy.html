Privacy Policy  |  Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Battle AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application Battle AI (the "App") on iOS and Android devices.

By downloading or using Battle AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

The short version: Battle AI does not require you to create an account, does not collect your name or email address, and does not sell your personal data. Advertising partners and AI services may collect limited device and usage data as described below.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Category What It Includes How It's Used
Device Information Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers (IDFA/GAID) Ad targeting and frequency capping by advertising partners
Usage Data Games played, session length, in-app actions App analytics and improving gameplay experience
Ad Interaction Data Ad views, clicks, and completion events Ad performance measurement by advertising partners
Game State Data Board positions or game context when you request a hint Sent to the Claude AI API to generate a gameplay hint (not stored)
Local Preferences Sound settings, difficulty level, high scores Stored locally on your device only via AsyncStorage — never transmitted

We do not collect your name, email address, physical location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal information.

4. Advertising & Ad Choices

Battle AI is free to play and is supported by advertising. Ads may be personalized based on your device identifiers and usage patterns by our advertising partners (Google AdMob and AppLovin MAX).

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track." You can also enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising.
  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

Opting out of personalized ads does not remove ads from the App — you will still see non-personalized ads.

We display the following ad formats: banner ads during gameplay, interstitial ads between games, and rewarded video ads when you choose to watch one in exchange for a gameplay hint.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Battle AI is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used Battle AI in a way that provided personal information, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take steps to remove that information.

If you are located in the European Union, the minimum age is 16 unless the laws of your country specify a lower age of digital consent.

6. Data Storage & Retention

Battle AI stores your game preferences and high scores locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Data collected by third-party advertising and analytics partners is retained according to their own data retention policies (linked in Section 3 above).

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell personal data).
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

Since Battle AI does not collect personal data on our own servers, most personal data subject to these rights is held by our third-party advertising partners. You may exercise your rights directly with those partners using the links in Section 3, or contact us and we will assist you.

California Residents (CCPA): We do not sell your personal information. Advertising partners may share device identifiers for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may opt out as described in Section 4.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the App and your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device software up to date.

Your continued use of Battle AI after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
